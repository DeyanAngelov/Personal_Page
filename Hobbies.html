Everybody has a Hobby!

  • Reading ▾

    • I enjoy reading since I was a small kid, some of my happiest memories are connected with speed reading an interesting book. Its by far my oldest hobby, if one could even call reading a hobby. Here I will list some of my favorite authors that are more wildly popular: J.R.R. Tolkien, I love and everything Middle Earth. Tolkien is simply the best and everyone else in the genre is just walking in his footsteps. Every other fantasy story is pretty much majored to The Lord, but none has yet reached its glory.
      Frank Herbert is another big favorite of mine. I think is by far the best Sci Fi book ever made. Dune is for the Sci Fi genre, what The Lord of the Rings is for the Fantasy genre and I cant think of a bigger compliment then that. The ideas of Frank Herbert are just wayyyyyyyy ahead of his time and even today they are still mind-blowing.
      Sir Arthur Conan Doyle and his holds a very special place in my hearth, as The Hound of the Baskervilles was one of the first serious books I've read at the age of 8 (for the first time at least). Ever since I have been fascinated by the use of logic and deduction of Mr Sherlock Homes. Soon after I finished everything else written about the Greatest Detective, the other 3 major story books and every short story I could find (to my knowledge I've read them all but I could be wrong).

  • Gaming ▾

    • Another hobby of mine is PC gaming. I started playing when I was around 10 years old, with few friends of mine. We played turn-based strategies, shooters and later, real-time strategies. Those were the days of my teen years with long LAN days and nights. Sadly they are all now a thing of the past and today I play more or less only 3 games: The first game I ever played and that I will probably play forever is Heroes 3 for me is the immortal game, I have few friends with whom, even today, we group up and play on one PC. Sadly as the years pass it gets harder and harder to find time, when all of us are free for a big, long, proper Heroes game, but this does not stop us trying.
      The game that I play the most for the past 7-8 years is I am a big fan of the Warcraft universe since the times of Warcraft 2. So when WoW came out it was only a matter of time till I started playing. I have been in a really nice guild for the last 5 years, we are called The Hungering cold and the name of my character is Xaav.
      The last game I play from time to time is I started playing Diablo 3 when it first came out mainly because of the RMAH (Real Money Auction House) and it did play off. After the release of RoS and the end of RMAH, I continued to play the game because after the changes that were made to it, I found it to be quite fun as solo RPG game.

  • Man's Best Friend ▾

    • Last but not least comes the time I spend with my 4 legged friend Jack. This is by no means a hobby, but for the lack of a better word I will call it one. He is a 4 and a half years old english pointer, who enjoys eating my shoes and stealing what ever is inside the washing machine, when no one is looking. I love spending time with him, playing or taking long walks, when possible, in the park or in the forests above Pernik's stadium. Here is a small album of Jack's photos that you can view! Go to Album
