Here I will tell you a bit about myself!

So from where to start?

My name is Deyan, I am 25 years old from Pernik Bulgaria, I went to the Professional High School of Economics in Pernik and later to the South-West University "Neofit Rilski" in Blagoevgrad. Last May (2014) I started as a student in InfoStart, a program made by Information Services (Информационно Обслужване) for IT education and now I have finished it successfully with a diploma from the Ministry of Education (МОН). I watched many different videos and tutorials since then and I continue to do so till this very day. I like the video and demo materials published by SoftUni, my humble opinion is that the efforts of Svetlin Nakov and his team deserves admiration. From SoftUni for example, I did all the homework tasks in their Web Fundamentals course and at the moment I am doing the same with the JavaScript Basics course. From everything I learned and came across so far in the big WWW world, I enjoy the most HTML5/CSS3, JS and PHP. Of course I have still much to learn (especially about JS and PHP but for now I want to focus on JS) but I am really eager to do so. So this is it pretty much in few words, if you want to learn more about me (and marvel at the glory of my website a little more) you can go and take a look at my Hobbies!

The Website is still work in progress. The plan is to have at least 4 major resolution break-points, optimized for: Chrome, Firefox and IE 10 (I hope).
